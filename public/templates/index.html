Hello...I'm Tim Carlock

I'm a full-stack web engineer and product designer who is passionate about innovation, entrepreneurship and product design/usability. I'm a native of the midwest and a recent transplant to the Bay Area. These days I spend a lot of time running, practicing martial arts and biking.

My Background

I started my career in the financial-services industry where I built an application which employed modern portfolio theory to recommend better allocations across various asset classes. I spent another five years in the financial-services industry where I built a product team before leaving to start a business-software company. I recently joined Learnist where we're building a product to help people more-easily learn new skills.

My Technology Preferences

I'm a big proponent of MV* frameworks on the client side and while I have experience with BackboneJS, my preferred framework is now AngularJS. Additionally, I use CSS3, Sass, CoffeeScript and jQuery. Some of my other tools include Sublime, Git, RSpec/FactoryGirl, easel.io and Balsamiq.
