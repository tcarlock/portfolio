Hello...I'm Tim Carlock

I'm a full-stack web engineer and design technologist who is passionate about technology, entrepreneurship and product design/usability. I'm a native of the midwest and a recent transplant to the Bay Area. These days I spend a lot of time running, practicing martial arts and biking.

My Work History

I started my career in the financial-services industry with A.G. Edwards (now Wells Fargo) where I built an application which employed modern portfolio theory to recommend better allocations across various asset classes. I spent another five years in the financial-services industry where I built a product team before leaving to start a business-software company. I recently joined Learnist where we're building a product to help people more-easily learn new skills.

My Technology Preferences

These days my preferred back-end stack includes PostgreSQL, Redis and Ruby on Rails. Although it's become crowded, I'm a big proponent of MV* frameworks on the client side and while I initially used BackboneJS, my preferred framework is now AngularJS. Additionally, I use CSS3, Sass, CoffeeScript and jQuery. Some of my favorite tools include Sublime, Pivotal Tracker, ack, Git, Batarang, RSpec and FactoryGirl.
